About Us

Ever since 1967, Focal Foods has been providing customers with the best food that money can buy. We have departments ranging from deli to produce and each department has their own selection of products. Focal Foods currently has locations in the state of Minnesota, but we are looking to expand to other states across America. Focal Foods also has recipes that the customers are free to make on their own should they feel the need to experience our food outside of the store. Focal Foods also promotes wellness in order for people to understand how important it is to live a healthy lifestyle. Check our news page to see what events are coming up. Happy shopping!
